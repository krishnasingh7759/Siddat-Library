What Is Siddat Library?

Siddat Library is a peaceful and welcoming educational space where students, professionals, and lifelong learners come together to read, study, and grow intellectually. Located in Khutauna Bazar, Madhubani, Siddat Library is committed to fostering learning, focus, and continuous improvement.

Using Siddat Library is straightforward:

  2. Select books from our diverse collection
  3. Use the study hall to read and prepare
  4. Return books on time and renew when needed
